📊 BUSINESS RULES IMPLEMENTED
================================================================================

PUMP CAPACITY:
  • 50,000 litres per runtime hour (FIXED)

CROP-SPECIFIC WATER RATES (per tree per hour):
  • Coconut (P1E, P1W, P2E, P2W): 100 L/tree/hour
  • Jackfruit (JF): 60 L/tree/hour
  • Nutmeg (NM): 80 L/tree/hour (FIXED, independent)

CALCULATIONS:
  • Runtime Hours = Total Minutes / 60
  • Total Water = Runtime Hours × 50,000 L
  • Water per Tree = Runtime Hours × Crop Rate

ZONE TREE COUNTS:
  • P1E: 125 trees
  • P1W: 110 trees
  • P2E: 135 trees
  • P2W: 128 trees
  • JF: 42 trees
  • NM: 35 trees

STATUS DETERMINATION:
  • Irrigated: Has 1+ record for date
  • No Record: Has 0 records for date
  • Partial: Has 2+ records for date
  • Issue: Reserved for future use

NUMBER FORMATTING:
  • Indian format: 1,23,456 L (not 123,456)
  • Time format: 3h 30m (not 3.5 hours)
  • All calculations use locale-aware functions
